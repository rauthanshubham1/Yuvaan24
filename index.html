ABOUT

Yuvaan, DTU's literary fest, began in 2017, attracting over 20,000 attendees with a mix of classical and modern literature. The next year, it expanded, drawing 40,000 with guests like Durjoy Datta and Simar Singh.

In 2019, Yuvaan evolved into the Literature and Film Festival (YLFF), reaching 50,000 attendees with guests like Manisha Koirala and partnerships with the LifeArt Festival.

Yuvaan 2020 featured a concert by Zaeden and Vivek Singh, a star-studded 'La Gossipe' segment, and Mandira Bedi's book launch.

In 2023, Yuvaan continued its legacy with a Kavi Sammelan featuring Azhar Iqbal and a Comedy Rumble event.

Looking ahead to Yuvaan’24, the aim is to become India's largest youth literary and film festival, emphasizing dialogue and empowering the youth.
